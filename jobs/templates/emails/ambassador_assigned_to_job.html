6. Demo Steps

  1. 1.Check-In
    • Upon arrival, ask for a manager or store staff.
    • Let them know you’re with Liquid Death and scheduled to do a sampling.
    • Confirm they have product in stock and decide where to set up
  2. 2. Purchase Products
    • Use the company card to purchase product for sampling (save receipts).
    • Buy 1 bag of ice and 1 (6-pack) of each flavor.
    • If SKUs are out of stock, note this in your recap.
  3. 3. Set Up
    • Display at least 2 cans (or cases) of each variety on your table.
    • Use ice buckets to keep cans cold.
    • Engage with consumers, share key talking points, and take photos.
    • Please look for what the cost of a LD Multi-pack is & write that with the expo marker on the sign.
    • If you are doing multiple demos, we've included multiple signs in your kit. Only use one per demo.
  4. 4. Photos to Capture
    • Store exterior (front entrance)
    • Any Liquid Death signage (inside/outside)
    • Product on-shelf
    • Any floor displays/pallets of the product
    • Your demo setup (with you in uniform)
    • 6-8 photos of consumers posing
    • Photo of your itemized receipt(s)
  5. 5. Recap & Wrap-Up
    • Open the Spark App to complete your recap.
    • Check the recap form when you arrive so you know what photos/details are required.
    • Submit your recap before leaving (use the last 10 minutes of your shift to finalize)
    • Payment is only processed upon completion of the recap.
    • Leftover products should not be used for other demos. You may take them home or dispose of them.
